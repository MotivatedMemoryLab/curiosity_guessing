Instructions

Welcome to the Video Guessing Game!

In this the task, you will be asked to watch animated videos of single-continuous line drawings. Each drawing will start with a blank screen and over time the drawing will slowly reveal itself. Each video is between 15 to 30 seconds long.

We are interested in knowing how you experience these videos. We will ask you a series of questions both throughout and after each video. We are interested in knowing your thought process throughout watching these videos.

You will watch a minimum of 25 videos in this task. If you find that you would like to watch more, you can opt to do so. We estimate that it will take about 25 minutes to complete. After you complete this part, you will be directed to Qualtrics where you will complete 2 surveys that should take about 5 minutes total.

If you are ready to begin, advance to the next screen. You will receive more specific instructions on how to do the task there.

As long as you try and pay attention you will be approved. If your answers are nonsensical you will be rejected.
