Instructions

Welcome to the Video Guessing Game!

In this the task, you will be asked to watch animated videos of single-continuous line drawings. Each drawing will start with a blank screen and over time the drawing will slowly reveal itself.

We are interested in knowing how you experience these videos. We will ask you a series of questions both throughout and after each video.

You will watch 30 videos in this task. We estimate that it will take between 30-35 minutes to complete. After you complete this part, you will be direct to a brief questionnaire (5 questions).

If you are ready to begin, advance to the next screen. You will receive more specific instructions on how to do the task there.
